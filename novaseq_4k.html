Novaseq VS 4000

Data are from NRG projects.

How:

  1. Took a sample that have been sequenced on 4000 twice, compare the overlapping reads and detect variations between the two batch of data (potential errors)
  2. Took a sample that have been sequenced on 4000 and NovaSeq, compare the overlapping reads and detect variations between the two batch of data (potential errors)

Result:

For each compariation, the number of reads generated for both batches are similar.

The true error rate would be half of the error_rate showing in the table.
